# Mind-Map
I developed my own mind map project which is pretty useful for doing what I want. That is why I would love to share this project with other people in order to use them and have a look at them to understand the structures freely.
<br />We have good features;

<br /><strong>Panning</strong>(hold middle button of your mouse on a blank area and drag the page),
<br /><strong>Zoom In/Out</strong>(scroll up/down),
<br /><strong>Changing the Theme</strong>(placed in "Settings" tab),
<br /><strong>Save</strong> (Ctrl+S),<br /><strong>Save As</strong> (Ctrl+Shift+S),<br /><strong>Save As PNG</strong> (no shortcut),<br /><strong>Import</strong> (Ctrl+I or shift + right click),
<br /><strong>Creating A Box</strong>(right click),
<br /><strong>Snapping Mode</strong>(S "on/off" or placed in "Settings" tab)
<br /><strong>Resizing Mode</strong>(R "on/off" or placed in "Settings" tab),
<br /><strong>Opening Boxes' Popup</strong>(left click on the box),
<br /><strong>Selecting</strong>(shift+left click),
<br /><strong>Multiple Selecting</strong>(shift+left click),
<br /><strong>Selecting Area</strong>(shift+left click(hold) on a blank area and drag your mouse),
<blockquote><em>You can click right button on a blank area to cancel your selections</em></blockquote>
<br /><strong>Deleting</strong>(X for selected by pressing shift),
<br /><strong>One By One Deleting</strong>(right click on boxes to delete one by one),
<br /><strong>Linking</strong>(shift+middle click on two different boxes),
<br /><strong>Linking With Direction</strong>(middle click on boxes),
<blockquote><em>The direction is from the first to the second box you click</em></blockquote>
<br /><strong>Opening Paths' Popup</strong>(left click on the path),
<br /><strong>Creating A New Box On Paths</strong>(right click on the path),
<blockquote><em>Also it's easy to remove</em></blockquote>
